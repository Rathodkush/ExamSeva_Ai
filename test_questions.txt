1. What is the capital of India?
Delhi

2. Explain the process of photosynthesis.
Photosynthesis is the process by which plants make food using sunlight, water and carbon dioxide.

3. Define Newton's first law of motion.
An object at rest stays at rest, and an object in motion stays in motion with the same speed and in the same direction unless acted upon by an unbalanced force.

4. Calculate the area of a circle with radius 5 cm.
Area = πr² = 3.14 × 25 = 78.5 cm²

5. What are the main components of a computer system?
The main components are CPU, memory, storage, input devices, and output devices.
