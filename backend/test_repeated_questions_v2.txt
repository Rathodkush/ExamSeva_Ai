(a) Force = mass × acceleration (b) An object at rest stays at rest (c) Action = reaction (d) Energy conservation

Question 10: State Newton's law of inertia
Newton's first law states that objects in motion tend to stay in motion unless acted upon by external force

Question 11: Explain Newton's first law of motion
The first law of motion states that a body at rest remains at rest and a body in motion remains in motion

Question 12: Define inertia according to Newton

Question 13: What is osmosis?
(a) Diffusion (b) Movement of water through membrane (c) Digestion (d) Respiration

Question 14: Define osmosis in biology
Osmosis is the movement of water molecules across a semipermeable membrane

Question 15: Explain the process of osmosis
Water molecules move from area of high water potential to low water potential through osmosis

Question 16: Describe osmosis with an example
In osmosis, water moves across membranes. Example: raisins swell in water
